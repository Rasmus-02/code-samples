# Clank robotics

A battlebots inspired game where the player builds and programs their own robot. I was responsible for the building system, saving, graphics and backend.

Made in collaboration with: William Heikkilä, Hugo Gillberg, Johannes Lindman, Axel Karlsson.

## Goals

- Learn C#
- Get better at writing clean code.
- Become familiar with backend development.
- Get an understanding of PostgreSQL, Docker and FastAPI.
- Collaborate in a larger team.
- Make a game that would be successful at ICT showroom.


## Achievements

- I learned C#.
- I got better at writing cleaner code.
- Got a better understanding of backend development.
- Learned how to use PostgreSQL, Docker and FastAPI.
- Clank-Robotics will represent what students are able to do with a DI-degree in computer engineering at DI-dagen at Åbo Akademi.
- Improved my ability to work as a part of a larger team.
- (ICT showroom has not yet happened, will update with results).
